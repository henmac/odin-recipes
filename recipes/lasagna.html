Description

The best Lasagna ever!!!

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 500 g minced beef
  • lasagna noodles
  • crushed tomatoes
  • bechamel

Steps

  1. turn on the oven
  2. brown beef in pan and set aside
  3. fry onions and garlic until onions are translucent
  4. add tomatoes to pan
  5. add beef to sauce
  6. assemble tomato sauce, noodles and bechamel in layers in pan and cook in oven for 30 min
