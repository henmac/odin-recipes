Description

The best Pizza ever!!!

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • pizza dough
  • crushed tomatoes
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. turn on the oven
  2. fry onions and garlic until onions are translucent
  3. add tomatoes to pan
  4. roll out dough into a thin circle
  5. assemble tomato sauce and mozzarella on top of dough
  6. cook in oven
